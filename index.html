My first and current car is a 2006 Chrysler Crossfire. This vehicle is filled with quirks, many of which I have a love/hate relationship with.

Cons:
- 2 Seater (Hard to drive people around)
- 18 MPG in city (if lucky)
- Premium fuel only
- At 6ft, my head has one inch of clearance
- Little to none trunk space
- Car seats only move forward, no space to go backwards
- Stuck in one seating position
- One cupholder
- Little to none interior storage
- Difficult to repair and maintain
