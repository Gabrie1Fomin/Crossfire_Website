Final Notes

This might be the best way to put it:
The Crossfire is the type of car meant for people who don't need to use it. Unless you have a garage and another practical vehicle, there should be little to no consideration of owning this vehicle.

Don't get me wrong. The car is fun, simple, and turns heads, but it will only have you shaking yours in the end.
