Final Notes
As a final word, I would like to stress that it was a mistake owning a sporty coupe as a first vehicle, much less the Crossfire.
If you are able to get this car as a first car, simply don't. The money put into the vehicle will not be worth what the car can do for you.
Parts, maintanance and upkeep will essentially light your wallet on fire.
